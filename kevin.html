Democratic Republic of Congo

The Democratic Republic of Congo is rich in culture, with over 200 ethnic groups with their own dialect contributing to its diversity.
Music plays a huge role in Congolese life, with styles like Rumba and Soukous enjoyed across Africa and beyond.

Kinshasa

Kinshasa is the cultural heart of the DRC, known for its music, fashion, and street art.
The city is a hub for musicians, producing famous artists like Fally Ipupa and Koffi Olomide.
